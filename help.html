Help & Tips

Offline use: You can generate PDFs offline. Sharing (WhatsApp/Email) will queue if offline and retry when you reconnect.

Quick Steps

  1. Fill vitals and notes.
  2. Tap Preview & Generate PDF to save a copy.
  3. Use Copy Summary for quick paste.
  4. Share via WhatsApp or Email. If offline, it will retry later.
