Victoria Nurse Help

Record patient vital signs and share quickly via WhatsApp, Email, or PDF. All data stays on your device by default.

How to Use

  • Tap Get Started on the landing page.
  • Fill in the patient’s vitals and medications.
  • Tap Preview & Generate PDF, Share via WhatsApp, or Share via Email.
  • Once sent, the form clears automatically to protect privacy.

Tips

  • Open the Settings (⚙ top-right) for footer branding or theme.
  • Offline? PDF generation still works, but sharing requires internet.
  • “Remember Me” stores nurse/patient names locally for 7 days.
