Victoria Nurse Help

Record patient vital signs and share quickly via WhatsApp, Email, or PDF. Data is kept client-side by default.

How to Use

  • Tap Get Started on the landing page.
  • Fill in the patient’s vitals and medications.
  • Tap Preview & PDF, Share WhatsApp, or Share Email.
  • Optional: toggle Remember on this device for nurse/patient names (7 days).
